## Caption

Did you know Amboseli has over 1,600 elephants?

Nestled in the shadow of Africa's highest peak, Amboseli National Park is elephant paradise. For more than 50 years, researchers have followed these remarkable herds, learning their family histories, movements, and social bonds across generations.

Here are five facts that make Amboseli unforgettable:

- Over 1,600 elephants roam the wider ecosystem.
- The Amboseli Elephant Research Project has studied them since 1972.
- Their famous red color comes from iron-rich dust used as natural sunscreen.
- Kilimanjaro-fed underground water keeps the swamps alive.
- Amboseli is still one of the best places to see Africa's big tuskers.

There is nothing quite like watching elephants move across open plains with Kilimanjaro rising behind them. Amboseli is the first safari chapter on our Kenya Family Safari, designed for families, photographers, and elephant lovers.
